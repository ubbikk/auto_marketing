The banned words aren't arbitrary. Research shows "delve," "leverage," "tapestry,"

NUMBER PATTERNS — THE CRITICAL TELL:
AI defaults to specific-but-round numbers: "47 projects," "200 clients," "73.2%."

over a thousand." Use human number patterns — approximations, hedged figures,
ugly real numbers. If you catch yourself writing a number that ends in 0 or 7,
rewrite it.
